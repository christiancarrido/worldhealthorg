ABOUT

Our team decided to choose a topic relevant to the issues that occupy our lives today. The COVID-19 pandemic prompted us to choose the WORLD HEALTH ORGANIZATION as our non-profit. WHO is an international effort to inform and provide valuable medical insight about the world's health conditions. Our goal as UI/UX designers was to help the organization present their information with appealing layouts and design, making it easier for users to comprehend and receive important points.

SITEMAP

The sitemap for our intended page flow was based heavily on the world health organization's actual website structure. Later in development, we changed some of its structure and content to maintain a minimal design for information displays

STYLE GUIDE

Our style guide focused on presenting a clean, minimal style that would efficiently represent the order and association towards medical cleanliness our organization is defined by.

USER PERSONA

A user persona created by Kenneth Lee helped us curate our design according to our target audience and demographic.

WIREFRAMES

This earlier wireframe focused heavily on layout and design motifs, and does not reflect the current style guide as it had preceded it. Changes were made to ensure the wireframe looked less generic and more minimalistic, sleek, and modern as seen in "Prototypes".

PROTOTYPES

These Hi-Fi prototypes included hover animations, carousels, and scroll to top functionalities to maximize the ease for the user.
